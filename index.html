Gulder

One of the beers with the strongest body that we can find among the multiple African Lagers. Medium taste with bitter hints and light sweetness, fine bubbles that allow a thicker head. It is without a doubt the tastiest Lager in Ghana.

Legend Scout

It differs from other stouts on the market by its complete brewing process that gives it a unique, richer flavor and long-lasting head.

Aguila

Beer with low alcohol content, aroma of malt and sweet corn, mild flavor where sweet corn is noticed and a touch of fruit.

Poker

Gold to orange color, with a fine durable foam cord. On the nose we find a beer with the aroma of malt and sweet corn. In the mouth, a smooth flavor with hints of hops and again that sweet corn.

Club Premium

Golden yellow, bright, with an infinity of rosaries and white foam, with a fine bubble and medium persistence, on the nose it shows subtle notes of yeast and soft aromas of cereals, barley and rice. Of low intensity in the mouth, it has a soft attack, slightly creamy and with a bitter touch.

Pilsner

Smooth, crystalline, filtered golden appearance, with medium-height, creamy head. Aroma of sweet grain, dried fruits and traces of honey that are mixed with the scent of spicy and herbal hops. Its flavor is mild, with a spicy character and sweet grain, which thoroughly tasting notes of nuts, bread and soft honey for uktimo with a dry herbal touch and outlined bitterness.
